FAQ

Do I need an account?
No. Just open a game and start playing instantly.

Where are my scores saved?
Scores are stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage. This means your data stays on your device. You can clear your scores anytime from the "All Games" or "Stats" pages.

Is it really free?
Yes, 100% free. We are a passion project dedicated to providing quality entertainment without cost. There is no real-money gambling or pay-to-win mechanics on our site.

Can you add [X] game?
We're always open to suggestions! Use our contact form to recommend a classic game you'd love to see on ArcadeHub. We focus on games that are lightweight and work well on the web.
